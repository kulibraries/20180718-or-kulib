This workshop, based on the Library Carpentry curriculum, can help you:

  • automate repetitive, boring, error-prone tasks
  • create, maintain and analyse sustainable and reusable data
  • work effectively with IT and systems colleagues
  • better understand the use of software in research
  • and much more...

For more information on what Library Carpentry teaches and why, please see the paper "Library Carpentry: software skills training for library professionals".
